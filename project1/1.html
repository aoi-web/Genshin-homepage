ABOUT

Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG by miHoYo, set in the fantasy land of Teyvat. Players explore, solve puzzles, and engage in elemental combat using a variety of characters with unique elemental abilities. The game emphasizes exploration, character progression, and stunning anime-style visuals.

CHARACTERS

Genshin Impact offers diverse characters, each tied to one of seven elements. They wield unique weapons and abilities, allowing players to combine elemental reactions for strategic combat and exploration.
